# Ruleset

## Objective
Win by Checkmating your opponent's King — if they cannot remove their King from Check before their turn ends, they lose.

## Components
- 54 cards: 52 in the shared deck, 2 King cards set aside (one per player)

## Board
5 lanes × 4 rows. Each player owns the 2 rows on their side. One piece per space. Adjacent means directly left, right, forward, or backward — not diagonal.

## Setup
1. Each player places their King in the centre lane of their back row.
2. Shuffle the 52 cards into a shared draw pile.
3. Each player draws 5 cards.
4. Before the first turn, each player may discard any number of cards and draw the same number.
5. Randomly determine who goes first.

---

## Turn Structure

### 1 — Draw
Draw 1 card. If the deck is empty, shuffle the discard pile into a new draw pile first.

### 2 — Actions
Take **2 actions**. Each action is one of:
- **Place** a piece onto any empty space on your side.
- **Play** a Control, Buff, or Debuff card.
- **Cycle:** discard 1 card from your hand and draw 1 card.
- **Direct** a placed piece to move, attack, or use its Sacrifice ability.

Each piece may be directed **once per turn**, not on the turn it was placed or enslaved.

**Command** and **Disrupt** are free — they do not cost an action and may each be played once per turn at any point during your turn.

### 3 — Check
If your King is in Check, remove it before your turn ends or you lose.

### 4 — Discard
Discard down to 5 cards.

---

## Movement and Attacks
- Pieces are placed and move only on your side of the board.
- Attacks reach the entire board. The attacking piece does not move.
- When a piece attacks, the target is sent to the discard pile.
- All pieces block attacks from passing through their space.
- Pieces cannot attack friendly pieces.

---

## Check
Your King is in Check when an opponent's piece can attack it. To remove Check:
- Move the King to a safe space
- Place or move a piece to block the line of attack
- Destroy or Fatigue the attacking piece
- Trigger Bodyguard on a piece adjacent to the King

Reactions cannot be used to prevent an action or card that removes the King from Check.

---

## Card Rules
When your opponent takes an action or plays a card, you may play 1 reaction before it resolves.

**Buffs and Debuffs** may target any non-King piece (yours or your opponent's). Each piece may hold 1 Buff and 1 Debuff at a time. A Silenced piece cannot receive Buffs. A piece with Protection cannot receive Debuffs.

All pieces removed from the board go to the discard pile.

---

## Cards

### Pieces

**King**
Moves and attacks 1 space in any direction. Cannot have Buffs or Debuffs attached.

**Pawn**
Moves forward 1 space. Attacks 1 space diagonally forward.
*Sacrifice: Remove this piece and draw 1 card.*

**Knight**
Moves and attacks in an L-shape (2 spaces in one direction, 1 perpendicular). Ignores blocking pieces.
*Sacrifice: Remove this piece and draw 2 cards.*

**Bishop**
Moves and attacks diagonally, any distance.
*Sacrifice: Remove this piece and draw 2 cards.*

**Rook**
Moves and attacks horizontally or vertically, any distance.
*Sacrifice: Remove this piece and draw 2 cards.*

**Queen**
Moves and attacks in any straight or diagonal direction, any distance.
*Sacrifice: Remove this piece and draw 2 cards.*

---

### Control

**Reposition**
Move one of your placed pieces to any empty space on your side.

**Swap**
Swap the positions of two of your placed pieces.

**Command** *(Free. Once per turn.)*
Gain 1 extra action this turn.

**Disrupt** *(Free. Once per turn.)*
Your opponent has 1 fewer action on their next turn.

**Dispel**
Remove one Buff or Debuff from any piece.

**Purge**
Remove all Buffs and Debuffs from all pieces.

**Return**
Return one of your placed pieces to your hand and remove all its Buffs and Debuffs. Cannot target the King.

---

### Buffs

**Enslave**
When this piece captures, place the captured piece in any empty space on your side (or discard it if none), discarding any Buffs or Debuffs it carries. It faces your opponent's side and cannot be directed this turn. Discard Enslave.

**Shield**
The next time this piece would be captured, discard Shield instead.

**Bodyguard**
When a directly adjacent friendly piece is attacked, you may sacrifice this piece to cancel the attack. This is not a reaction.

**Protection**
Discard any Debuff from this piece. This piece cannot receive Debuffs while Protection is attached.

---

### Debuffs

**Pin**
This piece cannot move.

**Fatigue**
This piece cannot attack.

**Silence**
Discard any Buff attached to this piece. This piece cannot receive Buffs while Silence is attached.

---

### Reactions

**Intercept**
Cancel one action or card as it is played, before it resolves.

**Reversal**
When a Buff or Debuff is played targeting a piece, redirect it to any other non-King piece that can legally receive it.
